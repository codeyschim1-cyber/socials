## Key Patterns & Insights
1. **Shares drive growth**: Posts with high share counts correlate directly with follower gains. The audience shares content as "guides" — places to visit, things to check out.
2. **Non-follower reach is massive**: 80-96% of viewers are non-followers — content is discovery-oriented.
3. **Bold superlative hooks work**: "BIGGEST", "BEST", "GIANT" consistently outperform neutral hooks.
4. **Location-specific content wins**: Tying content to a specific place (NYC, specific store names) drives saves and shares.
5. **Longer videos (35-49s) retain better**: 35-49 second reels have the best skip rates vs. typical.
6. **Cross-platform (IG + FB) amplifies**: Many posts get significant FB distribution (sometimes more than IG).
7. **Designer/luxury vintage is a breakout category**: The Ralph Lauren store video (926K views, 84K interactions) shows massive appetite for high-end vintage.
8. **Event content creates series**: Pre/during/after event structure drives sustained engagement.
9. **Average skip rate is ~35-40%** for top content vs. platform typical of 45-60%.
10. **Save-heavy content**: Audience treats content as reference material — store guides, location lists, price comparisons.
